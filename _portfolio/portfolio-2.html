--- title: "Revisiting Definable Categorical Equivalence" excerpt: "Hudetz (2019) has proposed a criterion of theoretical equivalence which aims to integrate model- and category-theoretic techniques of theory comparison. The idea is that category-theoretic tools relate the 'external' content of theories and model-theoretic tools relate 'internal' content. In this article I demonstrate that model- and category-theoretic tools for theory comparison are more closely related than the internal/external distinction suggests. In particular, I show how Hudetz's notion of a reconstruction functor can be reconstrued as an interpretation between theories and use this observation to demonstrate how manipulating the arrow structure of a model category can be recast in a model-theoretic framework." collection: portfolio ---
