I am a Ph.D. student in the [Department of Logic and Philosophy of Science at the University of California, Irvine](https://www.lps.uci.edu/). My work is primarily in mathematical logic and philosophy of mathematics. I have secondary interests in philosophy of physics and formal epistemology.

My research focuses on developing and scrutanizing techniques for theory comparison. In particular, I'm interested in comparing different languages used to develop foundational frameworks for mathematics (e.g. set theory, type theory, and category theory).
